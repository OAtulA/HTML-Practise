Top three activities to do at Ranchi

Take a Dip at Jonha Falls


For nature enthusiasts, a casual outing to Jonha Falls accounts for a day filled with sunbathing, picnicking and perhaps grabbing a few mouthfuls of jalebis at the local fair

Picnicking on Tagore Hill

Elusive Crocodile Breeding Grounds at Muta


Popular for its breeding rotations for crocodiles, here tourists can catch a glimpse of the gigantic reptile in the marshy waters of the evergreen Bhairavi rivulet.
